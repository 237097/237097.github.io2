Yūgi origami and Girei origami

Origami can be divided into stylized ceremonial origami (Girei) and and recreational origami (Yūgi). We are going to focus on the latter since generally only recreational origami is recognized as origami. Nevertheless we will cover more about the history of both here.

Different kinds of paper
